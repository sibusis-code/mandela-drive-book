## IMPORTANT

Do NOT simply generate the entire website at once.

First, think like a Creative Director.

Develop a complete visual and content strategy before writing any code.

Then create a detailed implementation plan.

Only after the plan has been approved should you build the website one section at a time.

Each section must be production-ready, polished, responsive, accessible and visually exceptional before moving to the next.

The final result should look like it was designed by one of the world's best digital agencies, while remaining authentic to the HM Pitje Foundation's mission and branding.
